Introduction

District boundary shapefile contain district boundaries for Rwanda since 2006. The shapefile was created in 2006 and updated by the 2012 Census mapping. The census mapping statred in 2011 and ended in 2012, where a team of 80 field staff collected census and administrtive boundaries up to the lowest administrative level which is "Umudugudu". Boundaries were adjusted in the GIS lab using te 2008-2009 Orthophoto taken by the Rwanda Natural ressource Authority. 
Rwanda has thirty District located inside four Province and Kigali City,its geographic distribution
is different among these province,Kigali City has three Districts,Eastern Province has seven District,Northern
Province has five districts,Southern province has eight districts and Western Province has seven Districts.

Data source

District boundary data have been produced by NISR in 2006 from 2001 administrative boundary shapefile created by the 2002  Population and Housing Census.  

Attributes 
Prov_ID: Contains one digit unique identifier for each province
Prov_Name: Contains the province name in English
Dist_ID: Contains the two digit unique identifier for each district. The first digit is for province and the second one for district inside a province
District: Contains the district name (30)
